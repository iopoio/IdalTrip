# 이달트립 v2 (IdalTrip) - UI/UX Design Requirements

## Overview
A premium editorial travel magazine-style Korean app that automates travel course generation based on region, date, and departure city.

## Core Principles
- **Language:** Korean (한국어)
- **Typography:** Modern Korean web fonts (Pretendard recommended).
- **Style:** Premium, clean, photo-rich, mobile-first (390px).
- **Device:** Mobile.

## Screens to Design

### 1. Home (Search-first)
- **Goal:** Effortless and inspiring search.
- **Key Elements:** Minimal header, Hero Search Card (Region chips, Date picker, Departure input), "Recommended Destinations" grid with status badges.

### 2. Place Selection
- **Goal:** Review and customize AI-recommended spots.
- **Key Elements:** Trip options summary bar (Departure, Transport, Duration), AI Insight Banner, Vertical list of place cards with type badges and Kakao ratings, Fixed bottom bar with selection count and CTA.

### 3. Course Result
- **Goal:** Visualizing the final itinerary.
- **Key Elements:** Hero Summary Card (Total time, cost, stops), Structured vertical timeline with photo-rich cards, travel time info, and "Kakao Navi" buttons. Fixed bottom bar for reshuffling or sharing.
